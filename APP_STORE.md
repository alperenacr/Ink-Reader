## Description (4000 char max)

Inkread brings the focused reading experience of a dedicated e-ink device to your iPhone and iPad — without the extra hardware.

**The e-ink feeling you love, on the screen you already have.**

Whether you're deep into a novel or working through a PDF, Inkread strips away every distraction and puts the text front and center. No social features, no bookstore, no subscription. Just you and your books.

**Import anything.**
Drag in your EPUB or PDF files directly from Files, iCloud Drive, or any app that supports sharing. Your library lives on your device — no cloud required, no account needed.

**Read your way.**
Switch between classic page-turning mode with satisfying haptic feedback, or infinite scroll for long-form reading sessions. Your preference is saved per book.

**Typography that respects the text.**
Four carefully tuned themes — E-Ink, Sepia, White, and Dark — each designed to reduce eye strain across different lighting conditions. Pair with Serif or Sans-Serif fonts at any size, with adjustable line spacing. Your settings apply instantly across your entire library.

**Never lose your place.**
Inkread remembers exactly where you left off in every book. Bookmark any page with a tap, highlight passages in four colors, and add personal notes to any selection. Your annotations are stored privately on your device.

**Find anything, instantly.**
Search within any book to jump straight to the passage you're looking for. Navigate chapters with the built-in table of contents. Sort your library by recently opened, title, author, or date added.

**iPad + Apple Pencil.**
On iPad, annotate directly on the page with Apple Pencil. Your handwritten notes are saved per page and persist across reading sessions.

**Spotlight Search.**
Find any book in your library directly from the iOS home screen search.

---

**Privacy first.**
Inkread has no network connection, no analytics, no advertising, and no account. Your books and annotations never leave your device.

**Style:** Minimal, clean, premium. Similar energy to iA Writer or Reeder.
